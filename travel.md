# Travel

Most visitors will fly to [Dublin](https://www.dublinairport.com/) even though
[Shannon airport](https://www.shannonairport.ie/) is geographically closer to Galway.
[Citylink](https://www.citylink.ie/)
operates frequent and reliable bus services from Dublin Airport to Galway
city. Advance booking is recommended.

# Stay

Galway is a popular [tourist destination](https://www.discoverireland.ie/galway),
and we therefore encourage participants to book their accommodation early.
At the time of writing, the University of Galway's
[campus accommodation](https://stay.universityofgalway.ie/)
provides an affordable and conveniently located alternative to standard
platforms such as
[Booking.com](https://www.booking.com/) and [Airbnb](https://www.airbnb.ie/).
