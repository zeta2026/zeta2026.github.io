The Sixth International Workshop on Zeta Functions in Algebra and Geometry
will take place at the University of Galway (Ireland) from 8–12 June 2026. 
The scientific programme will feature invited surveys and research talks, as
well as contributed talks and a poster session. Thematic pillars will include:

* Igusa-type (local, topological, motivic) zeta functions 
* Asymptotic and enumerative algebra 
* Multiple zeta values and motives 
* Connections with mathematical physics 

Further details about contributed talks, posters, and registration will follow
in due course.
